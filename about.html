Twigmax Animator is a 2d modeling and stickfigure animation program that I have written over the past couple of months, not as a commercial endeavor, but because I love programming. While it is far from a complete professional level application like Flash, I believe that it has enough capability to be of use to others.

Version v1.2 beta is now available. Twigmax Animator has come a long way since the first release called v1.0 beta. There are still many areas that need improvement, primarily the speed and performance. I don’t plan on stopping there, but it’ll be a nice milestone along the way.
